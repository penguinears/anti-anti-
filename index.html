Anti Anti-Gooners

On this site, we celebrate gooning as more than just a pastime — it’s a lifestyle, a mindset, and a vibe. The so-called “anti-gooners” don’t get it: they see obsession, we see dedication; they see wasted hours, we see transcendent focus. To goon is to lean fully into pleasure, to embrace the absurd intensity of the moment, and to laugh at anyone too uptight to understand. Being pro-goon isn’t just about the act itself — it’s about choosing joy, silliness, and unapologetic self-indulgence in a world that constantly tells you to tone it down.
